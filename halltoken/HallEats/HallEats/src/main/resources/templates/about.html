About FoodStaff

Welcome to the FoodStuff project. FoodStuff is a revolutionary solution designed to make the process of collecting hall tokens faster and more efficient.

Our project aims to streamline the token collection process for students residing in the university's halls as well as those living off-campus. With FoodStuff, students can quickly and conveniently collect their hall tokens, saving them precious time.

FoodStuff was developed by a dedicated team of students from Information and Communication Technology Department of Mawlana Bhashani Science and Technology University. Below, you can meet the key members of our team:
